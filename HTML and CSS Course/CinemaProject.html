About Ashley Cinemas

Located just 20mi from London, in the heart of Diagon Alley

Our theatre has been open since 1930. Good cinema and your comfort is our top priority!

Accomodation

All of our auditoriums are equipped with brand new state of the art Smell-O-Vision®.

Ticket price includes reserved seating in any one of our plush recliners, a cashmere throw, single use socks and 3D spectacles. For your convenience all concessions and drinks, including top shelf liquor from our lounge, can be delivered to your seat.

Our auditoriums are climate controlled to a pleasant 73° year round.

Ashley Cinemas is a 21+ facility
